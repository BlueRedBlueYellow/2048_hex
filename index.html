Merge the tiles and get to the final tile!

Keep going Try again

If you're on PC, use W, E, A, D, Z, X to move the tiles. If you're on mobile, then swipe!

When two tiles with the same player collide, they merge into one! Try to do this as many times as possible to get to the end, or at least a high score.

You get a game over when you can't move any more tiles.

Hex version by Rudradev Basak. Based on 2048 by Gabriele Cirulli and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer. Forked from Glukascewic1's 1_hex.
